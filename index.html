About SleepLean

SleepLean is a dietary supplement made with a combination of natural ingredients. The formula includes plant extracts, fibers, and nutrients traditionally associated with balance, rest, and overall well-being. Here are some of the main components:

  • Valerian: traditionally used to promote relaxation and support restful nights.
  • Hops: commonly found in teas, associated with rest and balance.
  • Griffonia (5-HTP): African plant known for supporting emotional well-being and sleep quality.
  • Berberine: a plant compound studied for its potential metabolic support.
  • Blue Spirulina: a natural source of nutrients and antioxidants.
  • Black Cohosh: traditionally used during women’s life transitions.
  • Lutein: an antioxidant linked to vision and skin support.
  • Inulin: a prebiotic fiber that supports digestive health.
