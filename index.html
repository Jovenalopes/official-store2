Understanding SleepLean

SleepLean is a dietary supplement made with a proprietary blend of 8 plant-based and natural ingredients, traditionally used to support general wellness. Each component has a history of use in areas such as relaxation, digestion, and overall balance.

Main Ingredients

  • Valerian Root – Traditionally associated with relaxation and calmness.
  • Humulus Lupulus (Hops) – Commonly used in teas, linked to relaxation and rest.
  • Griffonia Simplicifolia (5-HTP) – Natural source of 5-HTP, connected to serotonin production.
  • Berberine – Plant extract studied for potential metabolic support.
  • Spirulina Blue – Nutrient-rich algae valued for antioxidant content.
  • Black Cohosh – Traditionally used in women’s health during midlife transitions.
  • Lutein – Antioxidant associated with eye and skin health.
  • Inulin – Prebiotic fiber that contributes to digestive wellness.
